# Apple Contacts Changelog

## [Initial Release] - {PR_MERGE_DATE}

- Search, create, edit, and delete Apple Contacts from Raycast
- Split-pane layout with alphabetically-sectioned list and rich detail panel
- Detail panel shows photo, phone numbers, emails, addresses, birthday, job title, and notes
- Create contacts with `⌘N`, edit with `⌘E`, delete with `⌃X`
- Quick actions: compose email, call, open in Contacts.app, copy phone/email/name
- Fast two-phase loading: instant list, on-demand detail
